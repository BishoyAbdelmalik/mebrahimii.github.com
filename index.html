Mahdi Ebrahimi is an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at the California State University, Northridge. He obtained his doctoral degree in the field of data management in the Big Data Research Laboratory at Wayne State University. His research focuses on big data management, analysis and security. He has developed several metaheuristic optimization strategies for cloud data placement and workflow scheduling. He has published several research articles in peer-reviewed international journal and conferences, including IEEE conference on big data, big data congress and big data computing services and applications. He is a member of IEEE and ACM.

His primary research interests include Big Data Management and Analysis, particularly Big Data Management using workflow management system; Data placement in workflows.
